Delete Your CycleSync Account

You can request deletion of your CycleSync account and associated data.

How to delete your account

  1. Open the CycleSync app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap Delete Account and confirm

Alternative method

If you can’t access the app, email: 3dpeektech@consultant.com from the address linked to your CycleSync account.

Data deleted

  • Account profile and identifiers
  • Cycle tracking entries
  • App usage data linked to the account

Processing time

Deletion requests are processed within 7 days.
